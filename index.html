But as good as their relationship was she rejected him as quickly as she could !!! Snaik as angry as he was disappeared for some time... One day when T'Rek was helping the creatures he was friendly with, something felt wrong.

When he came home trying to find Dragonia, he found a letter saying he should get his girl back from Snaik He ran as quickly as he could just to find his beloved Dragonia DEAD.

T'Rek was lost in his mind while beeing attacked by Snaik for some time, already being on the shrink of death he remembered Dragonia's laugh, he went berserk and ripped Snaik to pieces. T'Rek was not seen since... to be continued???
